## 🌟 About Us

**SuMaOnix** is an MSME-registered EdTech startup on a mission to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry experience. We empower **school students**, **college students**, and **developers** by providing hands-on internships, industry-relevant courses, and cutting-edge tech services — all under one roof.

We are backed by a passionate team of **Data Science & AI professionals** who believe that every student deserves access to quality tech education and practical exposure.

> *"We don't just teach tech — we help you live it."*

---

## 🎯 Our Vision

To become India's most student-centric EdTech platform — where learning meets doing, and potential meets opportunity.
